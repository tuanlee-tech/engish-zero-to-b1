# Unit 4 - are you doing? (Present Continuous Questions)

## A - Grammar

### Positive → Question

| Positive                | Question                              |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| I **am**                | **am** I **doing** … ?                |
| he / she / it **is**    | **is** he / she / it **doing** … ?    |
| we / you / they **are** | **are** we / you / they **doing** … ? |

**Examples:**

- What are you doing?
- Are you feeling OK?
- Is it raining?
- Why are you wearing a coat?
- What’s Paul doing?
- What are the children doing?
- Where’s Emily? Where’s she going?
- Who are you waiting for? Are you waiting for Sue?

## B - Word order

**is/are + subject + -ing**

| Question word | is/are | subject      | -ing           |
| ------------- | ------ | ------------ | -------------- |
|               | Is     | he / Ben     | working today? |
| Where         | are    | they         | going?         |
|               | Are    | those people | going?         |

**Note:**

- Is Ben working today? (not Is working Ben today?)
- Where are those people going? (not Where are going those people?)

## C - Short answers

**Yes answers:**

|      | am    | is                | are                  |
| ---- | ----- | ----------------- | -------------------- |
| Yes, | I am. | he / she / it is. | we / you / they are. |

**No answers:**

|     | am       | is                   | are                     |
| --- | -------- | -------------------- | ----------------------- |
| No, | I’m not. | he / she / it isn’t. | we / you / they aren’t. |

**Examples:**

- Are you going now? → Yes, I am.
- Is Ben working today? → Yes, he is.
- Is it raining? → No, it isn’t.
- Are your friends staying at a hotel? → No, they aren’t. They’re staying with me.
